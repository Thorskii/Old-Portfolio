    Hobbies

    As mentioned previously, I have a few hobbies I do on my free-time. One of which is photography. I've been interested in photography pretty much my whole life as I've loved capturing the beautiful elements of life not everyone gets to see. I take great pride in my photography work and I only showcase my absolute favorite. All of the photos on my website are taken and edited by me!

    Another one of my 'less interesting' hobbies is playing video games. Video games, like many others, was my entrance into the world of technology as a child. I had always dreamed of making my own game, with my own story, and with my own mechanics. I've met numerous lifelong friends through gaming and it holds a special place in my heart.
